5 reasons to visit London

London theatre

London offers some of the world's best theatre, from world-famous musicals to West End shows and cutting-edge plays. Be sure to book ahead and take advantage of cheap London theatre ticket deals and offers.

Best views in London

London's famous skyline continues to evolve. There are plenty of places to view the iconic skyline along the river, but make sure you take in a panoramic view of London from up high at locations such as View from The Shard or the London Eye.

London's River Thames

The Thames provides a stunning backdrop to many of the city's top tourist attractions. River bus services and river tours are great ways to beat the traffic and enjoy wonderful views. Don't forget London's canals as well, including Regent's Canal and Little Venice.

Buckingham Palace

You can visit Buckingham Palace and see where the Queen lives, and if you are lucky enough you can see the Royal family in person. Buckingham Palace is protected with Queen's Guard, and you can take interesting pictures with them because they can't move.

London pubs

After a long day, you can sit and relax with bottle of good beer and enjoy in charms of great London pubs.There many historical pubs, some were around before Shakespeare, others are comparatively recent Victorian additions, but all have a fascinating story behind them.
